Overview of the Situation

- Dr. Lynne Kitei, a physician, saw and photographed a strange Orb from the balcony of her home. Her home is on a hillside, and from her balcony she has an extensive view of distant city lights.

- Yet, the city lights in the two photographs change in a way that could not have happened in just a few minutes. In a follow-up study, it was found that it takes several hours for such a change in city lights to occur.

- Dr. Bruce Maccabee, who noticed this change in the city lights, concluded that this was the first case of missing-time (the inexplicable disappearance of time normally experienced) and the first time it had been documented by physical evidence in the form of photographs.
